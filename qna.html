I answer some questions you might have.

Q: Will this be ported to other browser platforms i.e. Safari or Firefox?

A: I currently don't have any plans for that.


Q: How do I install sgen?

A: You will download a zip file. Extract the folder inside it. Then, go into your Chromium-based browser of choice i.e. Google Chrome or Edge Chromium. Then, if you're on Chrome, go to chrome://extensions or if you're on Edge, go to edge://extensions, for others, Google is your friend. Enable developer mode, hit load unpacked, load the extracted folder, and you're done!
